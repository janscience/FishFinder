### Display

[Adafruit 2.0" 320x240 Color IPS TFT Display](https://www.adafruit.com/product/4311) on SPI via ST7789 chip:

- MOSI1 (yellow): 11
- CS1 (orange): 10
- RST (green): 9

- SCK1 (white): 13
- D/C (blue) : 14
- BL (purple): 15

- GND (black)
- 3.3V (red)
